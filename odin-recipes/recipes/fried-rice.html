Fried Rice

Return to main page

This is a recipe commonly used in one of the most popular restaurants in Taiwan

    Ingredients
  • Eggs
  • Left over rice (plain/white)
  • Green vegetable of choice(cabbage/spinach/bok choy/etc.)
  • Meat of choice(chicken/pork/beef/shrimp/etc)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Others(personal prefference: corn/green onions/onions/etc)
  • Oil
    Steps
  1. Crack one egg and mix it into the cold rice
  2. Heat the oil on high in a large wok or frying pan
  3. Crack an egg into the pan
  4. Immediately after scrambled and cooked, add rice
  5. Cook meat in seperate pan
  6. Once rice reaches desired texture, add vegetables and cooked meat
