Lemon Green Tea

Return to main page

This is a great drink to enjoy on the warm days and it is a much healthier choice than store bought options

    Ingredients
  • Green Tea
  • Lemons
  • Optional: Sugar
  • Optional: Ice
    Steps
  1. Make the green tea and let it cool
  2. Juice a half lemon per glass
  3. Add lemon juice to the glass
  4. Add cooled tea to the glass
  5. Optional: add sugar and ice
  6. Stir (better results if shaken in a closed container)
  7. Enjoy!
