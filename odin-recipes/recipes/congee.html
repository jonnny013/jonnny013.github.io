Congee

Return to main page

This is an easy recipe that doesn't take much effort and is great for any meal of the day.

    Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Water
  • Shrimp
  • Clams
  • Green onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
    Steps
  1. Measure your rice and rinse it
  2. Use four times as much water to rice and add them to a pot
  3. Turn heat on to medium
  4. Stir every 5-10 minutes until the congee has reached your desired consistency
  5. Add other ingredients and cook thoroughly
  6. Enjoy!
